Welcome to Agrochain-HTS

Agrochain-HTS is a blockchain solution designed to bring transparency, traceability, and efficiency to the agricultural sector through the Hedera Token Service (HTS).

Agrovet Program

Agrovet is our specialized program focused on livestock health, modern dairy practices, and farmer education. It enables youth engagement in animal care, promotes best practices in breeding and feeding, and connects rural and urban markets for fair trade.
