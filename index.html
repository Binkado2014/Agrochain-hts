Agrovet — Livestock Health & Youth Training

Agrovet focuses on livestock health and youth training, connecting farmers with modern veterinary services and educational programs to improve productivity and well-being.

  • Animal Health: Vaccinations, care, and modern health management.
  • Youth Training: Education on cattle/sheep farming, milk hygiene, and processing.
  • Market Access: Connecting farmers with buyers via HTS-based platforms.
  • Tele-vet Services: Consultation with veterinary experts online.
  • Token Incentives (AGRO & AMG): Rewards for participation and compliance.
